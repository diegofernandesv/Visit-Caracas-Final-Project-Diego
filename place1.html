Quebrada Quintero

Quebrada Quintero is a hidden gem tucked away in the lush landscapes of El Ávila National Park. Known
only to a few adventurous explorers, this tranquil waterfall offers an unparalleled escape into nature.
Surrounded by dense greenery and accessible via a little-known trail, it feels like stepping into a secret
paradise. The crystal-clear waters cascade gently over the rocks, creating a serene atmosphere perfect for
those seeking peace and solitude. Its untouched beauty makes it a rare find, ideal for hikers who
want to discover a quieter side of El Ávila, away from the crowds.
