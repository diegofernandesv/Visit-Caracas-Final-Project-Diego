Essential Safety Tips
for Exploring El Ávila

1. Trail Safety

Stick to marked paths, respect wildlife, and avoid hiking alone. Always inform someone of your plans.

2. Weather Awareness

Check the forecast, dress appropriately, and stay hydrated. Avoid exploring during adverse weather.

3. Health Precautions

Know your limits, carry snacks and water, and wear sunscreen. Keep local emergency numbers handy.

4. Gear Essentials

Pack a first aid kit, flashlight, and other necessary gear. Safely secure camera equipment.

5. Tour Etiquette

Follow guides’ instructions, know the route, and use helmets when required.

6. Navigation & Communication

Bring a map/compass, have an emergency plan, and mark your route if needed.

7. Wildlife Awareness

Keep a safe distance from animals and use insect repellent.
