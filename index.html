About El Ávila

El Ávila National Park is a natural gem located in Caracas, Venezuela. It offers various hiking trails, waterfalls, and breathtaking vistas, making it a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts.

Beyond its natural beauty, El Ávila is a symbol of Venezuelan culture and pride. Whether you're exploring its scenic routes or simply enjoying the tranquility it offers, this iconic mountain connects visitors to the soul of Caracas.

Respect and Protect:
Sustainable Practices for Exploring El Ávila

  • Stay on marked trails to protect wildlife and vegetation
  • Pack out all trash, including food scraps
  • Avoid disturbing animals and keep a safe distance
  • Respect nature and leave no trace of your visit
  • Use eco-friendly products and minimize plastic use
  • Follow all local regulations and park guidelines
